ABOUT ME


Maryanne Middlemiss

Maryanne has worked in the Insurance Industry since 1980, starting in the typing pool at South British United in Burwood.

Since then Maryanne has progressed through the Industry, working for New Zealand Insurance in Rural Western Australia, then in the Allianz CTP Division and finally at Capricorn Risk Services. Maryanne is now an Authorised Representative of the Westcourt Group.
